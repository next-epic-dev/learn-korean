Privacy Policy

DramaKorean · Last updated July 2026

This policy explains what we collect when you join the DramaKorean waitlist and how we use it. We keep it short because our practice is simple.

What we collect

When you join our waitlist — through our website or a TikTok lead form — we collect the email address you provide, and, if you arrived from an ad, basic campaign information (such as the ad source) so we understand where interest comes from. We do not collect payment details at this stage.

How we use it

We use your email for one purpose: to tell you when DramaKorean's first episode is available and when your founding seat opens. We do not sell, rent, or share your email with third parties for their own marketing.

How it is stored

Waitlist emails are stored securely with our infrastructure provider. If you submit through a TikTok lead form, TikTok also processes your submission under its own privacy terms before it reaches us.

Your choices

You can ask us to remove your email at any time, and every email we send will include an unsubscribe link. To request removal directly, contact us at the address below.

Contact
